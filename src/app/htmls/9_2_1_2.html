954 Health Care Entities

210 Balance Sheet

50 Disclosure

General Note: The Disclosure Section provides guidance regarding disclosure in the notes to financial statements. In some cases, disclosure may relate to disclosure on the face of the financial statements.

General

> Expirations of Donor-Imposed Restrictions

50-1 Not-for-profit, business oriented health care entities are not required to make the disclosure required by paragraph 958-605-50-3 because, pursuant to paragraph 954-205-45-9, health care entities are prohibited from implying a time restriction that expires over the useful life of a donated long-lived asset.

> Assets Limited as to Use

50-2 When internally designated funds are reported separately from externally designated funds in accordance with paragraph 958-210-45-4, if the form of the assets is not evident from the description on the balance sheet, the form of the assets shall be disclosed in the notes to the financial statements.
